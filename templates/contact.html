Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to our most common questions. If you still need help, don't hesitate to contact us.

To book an appointment, log in to your student account, navigate to the "Book Appointment" section, select your professor, choose an available date and time slot, provide a brief description of the purpose of your meeting, and submit your request. You'll receive a notification once the professor confirms your appointment.

To cancel an appointment, go to "My Appointments" in your dashboard, find the appointment you wish to cancel, and click the cancel button. For rescheduling, you'll need to cancel the existing appointment and book a new one at your preferred time.

If you don't see any available time slots, it means the professor has not set availability for the selected date or all slots are already booked. Try selecting a different date or contact the professor directly to arrange an appointment outside their regular office hours.
