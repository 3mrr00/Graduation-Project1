Our Story

The Appointment System was developed as a graduation project by a talented team of students from the Egyptian E-Learning University (EELU) during the 2024-2025 academic year.

Recognizing the challenges faced by students and professors in scheduling meetings, our team set out to create an intuitive platform that would streamline the appointment process and enhance academic communication.

Under the guidance of distinguished faculty members, we've created a solution that addresses real-world problems faced by educational institutions.
